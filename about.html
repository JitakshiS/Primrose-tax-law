About the Firm

A FOCUSED PRACTICE

Primrose Tax Law is a Canadian tax law firm practising corporate tax and trust law for owner-managers. We handle corporate and trust reorganizations in collaboration with tax accountants across all nine common law provinces.

Primrose Watson, JD
Founder & Counsel · She/Her/Hers

I designed Primrose Tax Law to be a place where I can be the tax enthusiast I am. We work collaboratively with tax advisors and business owners to provide timely and efficient corporate tax planning legal services.

Accountants design the strategy. We implement the legal structure — without conflict, without competition, on instruction.
