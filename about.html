About the Firm

A FOCUSED PRACTICE
of one.

Primrose Tax Law is a Canadian tax law firm practising corporate tax and trust law for owner-managers. We handle corporate and trust reorganizations in collaboration with tax accountants across all nine common law provinces.

Primrose Watson, JD
Founder & Counsel · She/Her/Hers

I designed Primrose Tax Law to be a place where I can be the tax enthusiast I am. I wanted to build a firm around one principle: collaborate with accountants, never compete with them.

Tax advisors design the strategy. We implement the legal structure — the resolutions, the elections, the trust deeds — without conflict, without competition, on their instruction.

We work across all nine common law provinces from offices in Halifax, Toronto, Calgary, and Surrey. Every engagement is fixed-fee, every file handled with solicitor-client privilege where permissible.
