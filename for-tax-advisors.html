Sample Engagements

What a quote looks like.

Here are two real examples of quotes for the steps below. Legal fees include disbursements (registry fees).

Example A
Estate freeze with family trust
Family trust takes future growth of the operating company. Five-step engagement, common for business owners with an operating company.
# Step in the reorganization
1 Establish family trust with third-party loan
2 Increase Opco preferred shares
3 Section 86 freeze of Opco common shares
4 Family trust subscribes for Opco common shares
5 Dividend
Estimated total before tax $7,500
Example B
Estate freeze with Newco and S.85 rollover
Larger reorganization adding a Newco and a Section 85 rollover of personal property. Seven-step engagement.
# Step in the reorganization
1 Establish family trust with third-party loan
2 Establish Newco
3 Section 85 rollover for personal property
4 Increase Opco preferred shares
5 Section 86 freeze of Opco common shares
6 Family trust subscribes for Opco common shares
7 Dividend
Estimated total before tax $10,000

Tax (HST/GST) is added at billing. Each engagement is quoted on its own facts — complexity, jurisdiction, and document count will vary.
