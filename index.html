Common Questions
Frequently asked.
Will you compete for my clients?
No. I implement the legal structure your tax plan requires. I don't provide tax planning advice, and I don't solicit your clients. Your advisory relationship stays exactly as it is.
How does billing work?
Send me an anonymized tax plan to obtain a quote. Fixed fees, quoted upfront. You receive an implementation timeline and fixed fee within one business day of the referral.
Which provinces do you cover?
I work across Canada. I am called to the Bar in four provinces — British Columbia, Alberta, Ontario, and Nova Scotia — with offices registered for service in Halifax, Ottawa, Calgary, and Surrey. I practice in the other provinces (Saskatchewan, Manitoba, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Newfoundland & Labrador) based on the National Mobility Agreement.
How long does a file take?
Two to six weeks depending on complexity. Electronic signatures throughout means minimal disruption to the business owner’s schedule.
