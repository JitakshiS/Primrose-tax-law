A note on the recommendation

Concept 1 (Referral Brief) is my pick because it does the actual job of the SOW: capture referral inquiries. The other two build awareness; this one structures a referral. It's also the one tax accountants are most likely to share with each other, which extends the reach without paid distribution.

That said, the three are not mutually exclusive long-term. Start with one, layer the others later if the funnel needs more entry points.
